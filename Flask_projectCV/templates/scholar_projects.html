Scholar Projects

Master's Degree Dissertation - Benchmarking Distributed Deep Learning Frameworks for Healthcare Use Cases

Explored the efficacy of various distributed deep learning frameworks in healthcare applications, analyzing their performance with convolutional neural networks (CNN) and generative adversarial networks (GANs). Investigated datasets of varying sizes, from 500 to over 70,000 training samples, and evaluated different distribution strategies across distinct computing environments such as Cloud and HPC (High-Performance Computing).

Prevention of Auditory Diseases and Behavioral Pattern Detection through Captured Sound

Conducted research on preventing auditory diseases and detecting behavioral patterns through captured sound. Implemented Pyaudio to retrieve dB values and an online server for continuous monitoring of the recorded file. Upon detecting dangerously high values, the system sends notifications to mobile devices.

Liver Failure Prediction

Analysis and visualization of data from hepatic patients led to the prediction of liver failure with an accuracy exceeding 96%. Meticulously cleaned the data, including the removal of null values, and employed classification algorithms using Python and WEKA. Critical factors influencing the condition of liver disease in end-stage patients were successfully identified.

Bariatric Surgery Follow-up

Analysis of medical data related to bariatric surgery using Python, Excel, and PowerBi, including patient attendance and missed appointments for scheduled consultations. Ensuring adherence to expected norms for this type of intervention. Locally launched a website using Flask API with a user-friendly interface.
