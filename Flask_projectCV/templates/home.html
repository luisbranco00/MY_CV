Experience

Research Fellow on Health Promotion in Children and Families of Primary Schools in Social Vulnerability Conditions (BeE-school) - 2023

Extensive research was conducted to identify potential correlations among collected data, employing machine learning and deep learning tools such as WEKA, RapidMiner, and Tensorflow.

Data collected from school visits were integrated into the database. Essential metrics, including percentiles and Z-scores, were calculated and optimized for future use by simply modifying column names and required files. Possible relationships between socio-economic factors in a child's life and occurrences of hypertension or high heart rate were discovered.

This project enhanced practical skills in data science, enriching direct data analysis capabilities. It also expanded expertise in using diverse tools for highly specialized ETL processes within the research context. Additionally, it provided valuable insights into the factors influencing proper physical and emotional development in children during early education.
